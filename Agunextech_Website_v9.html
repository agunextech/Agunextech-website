Who We Are

We Make Technology Accessible for Every Business

Agunextech is a Nairobi-based technology company founded in 2025 with a clear mission, to make powerful technology accessible and affordable for every business in Kenya and across Africa.

We believe every business, regardless of size, deserves a strong digital presence and reliable technology infrastructure. From small enterprises building their first website to organisations requiring complete IT networking solutions, Agunextech delivers on every front.

The name Agunextech comes from our founder's identity. AGU from AGUNDA, a nickname given by close friends and university colleagues, and most notably by Professor Korongo, a dedicated and inspiring lecturer who taught Database Systems and Software Engineering. Her mentorship, encouragement and belief in our founder's potential played a significant role in shaping the technical foundation that Agunextech is built on today. NEXTECH means Next Technology, always moving forward.

ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS

Agunextech would not exist without the guidance, sacrifice and vision of two extraordinary individuals who shaped the founder's journey in the most profound ways.

Engineer Zack Orembe
Guardian, Field Mentor and Visionary

Engineer Zack Orembe invested in our founder from Primary School through to University. It was his far-sighted guidance that redirected AGUNDA ALLAN from Economics and Statistics into Informatics, the decision that gave birth to Agunextech.

Beyond academics, Engineer Zack Orembe took AGUNDA ALLAN as his personal apprentice, training him hands-on in Structured Cabling, Network and Wi-Fi Infrastructure, and CCTV and Security Solutions. He would take him to job sites, hand over full responsibility and trust that everything would be done right. It always was.

The practical technical skills that Agunextech delivers to clients today were not learned in a classroom. They were learned on real sites, under the patient and trusting guidance of Engineer Zack Orembe. His belief, sacrifice and vision are the true foundation of everything we build.

Professor Korongo
Lecturer, Database and Software Engineering

Professor Korongo was among the first to recognise the technical potential in our founder at university. Through her teaching of Database Systems and Software Engineering, she laid the academic and professional groundwork that Agunextech's technical expertise is built upon today.
